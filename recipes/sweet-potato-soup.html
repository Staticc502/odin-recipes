Sweet Potato Soup

Description

This sweet potato soup has a rich, sweet, and smoky flavor, with a tangy finish from apple cider vinegar. The texture is wonderfully smooth and creamy, which makes a single bowl feel really substantial.

Ingredients

  • 2 Sweet potatoes
  • 1 Apple
  • 1 medium Onion and 1 clove of Garlic
  • Coriander and Ginger to taste
  • Smoked Paprika
  • 2 tbsp Extra-virgin Olive Oil, 1 can of Cream, 3 tbsp of Butter
  • Chicken Broth
  • Apple Cider Vinegar
  • Sea Salt

Directions

  1. Saute the veggies: Cook the onion in a large pot until it softens (around 5 minutes). Add the sweet potatoes and apple and cook until they begin to soften, for another 8-10 minutes.
  2. Stir in the Seasonings: Add the garlic,ginger,coriander, and smoked paprika and stir until they're fragrant. Then mix in the apple cider vinegar, followed by the broth.
  3. Simmer: Bring the soup to a boil, cover the pot and simmer until the sweet potatoes are tender.
  4. Puree the Soup: Allow the soup to cool and transfer to a large blender and puree until smooth. Stir in the cream, season to taste and serve!
