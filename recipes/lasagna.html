Lasagna

Description

A rich and creamy whole-wheat pasta dish filled layer by layer with refreshingly fresh onions and garlic, lathered in a succulent sauce and topped with a beautiful cheese blend.

Ingredients

  • 2lb of lean minced beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 medium green or red pepper
  • 8 button mushrooms
  • 1 14oz. tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 2 dessertspoons of tomato sauce
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 10 sheets of easi-cook lasagna sheets
  • as much cheese as your heart desires (mozzerella, ricotta, parmesean)

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water per the recipe below
  2. Prepare the Meat Sauce: Cook beef with onion, mushrooms,and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce and tomatoes & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken
  3. Combine Cheese Mixture: Stir all cheese together in a bowl
  4. Layer & Bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until brown and bubbly
  5. Enjoy! Now prepare to enjoy your fresh and delicious italian dinner with family and friends!
